\[ \bar{x} \pm t_{\frac{\alpha}{2}, n-1} \times \frac{s}{\sqrt{n}} \]

Where:
- \( \bar{x} \) is the sample mean.
- \( t_{\frac{\alpha}{2}, n-1} \) is the t-score for a confidence level of \( 1-\alpha \) (95% in this case) with \( n-1 \) degrees of freedom.

- \( n \) is the sample size.
